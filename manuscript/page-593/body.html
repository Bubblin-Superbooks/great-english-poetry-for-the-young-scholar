### Hervé Riel.

"Hervé Riel" (by Robert Browning, 1812-89 is a poem for older boys. Here is a hero who does a great deed simply as a part of his day's work. He puts no value on what he has done, because he could have done no other way.   )

On the sea and at the Hogue, sixteen hundred ninety-two,
