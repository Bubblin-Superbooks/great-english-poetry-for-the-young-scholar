### Fairy Song.

Shed no tear! O shed no tear! The flower will bloom another year.
Weep no more! O, weep no more! Young buds sleep in the root's white core.
Dry your eyes! Oh! dry your eyes! For I was taught in Paradise
To ease my breast of melodies—
Shed no tear.

Overhead! look overhead! 'Mong the blossoms white and red—
Look up, look up. I flutter now
On this flush pomegranate
